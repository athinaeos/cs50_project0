One of the objectives of this website is to provide the visitor with material that enhances her knowledge of modern Greek history.

Knowledge of history is important because:

  • By knowing the mistakes of the past we avoid repeating them in the future.
  • We become better citizens as we learn about the institutions and the adventures of our democratic system of governance.
  • We get to appreciate the world as it develops and how it affects our country.
